Learn About Our Services
At Find your Housing we aim to make life easy for international students to find housing. We know that transitioning to a new country and a new environment is hard enough, and that students need to transition quickly into a home that will help them to focus on their studies. We especially understand the needs of students from the Middle East who may have specific preferences to live near places of worship and halal restaurants, in addition to being close to campus and living with roommates who share their same faith and culture. We do the work before students arrive to give them multiple housing options based on their preferences. We also book them a hotel that is conveniently accessible to campus while they finalize their housing arrangements. All of this makes their transition quick, painless, and efficient so they can focus on what they came to America to do: STUDY.

Who We Are

We are a team of professionals with experience in international student Academic Advisory for Middle Eastern Students, university teaching in the Middle East, US law, as well as real estate management in the USA. 

Our History

Our team of professionals have managed hundreds of apartments in different parts of the USA so we are very familiar with the real estate market. We have also advised hundreds of students from the Middle East to help them in their studies in the USA.
